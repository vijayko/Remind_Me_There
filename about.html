Remind Me There

Isn't it annoying to realize that you forgot to do your task while you were out, and were actually passing the location where you could do your work? "Remind me there" application is designed not to let your brain forget the task that you plan to do while you are out. In short, it's a location based reminder that reminds you to do your task while you are within the certain radius of the place.
